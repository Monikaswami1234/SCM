ASSET/INVENTORY TRACKING

Supply Chain Visibility provides a real-time picture of where the inventory is / assets are, when it will arrive, and how the supply chain is performing. Supply chain stakeholders across multiple functions access a single platform that combines updates from various partners with data to provide visibility, from source to customer delivery.

Improved responsiveness and faster collaboration help to leverage market opportunities and save costs.

SCM Connect offers various solutions to make your supply chain visible to all key process stakeholders. The different components of solution include a combination of hardware, software and Business Intelligence tools.

Our technology solution provides a network wide view of your inventories or assets across the supply chain. We offer seamless integration throughout the supply chain and visibility from production to the point of sale.

  • Lower inventory levels and safety stocks
  • Outsource logistics effectively
  • Improve lead times and performance
  • Reduce Slob inventory
  • Reduce business and supply chain risk
  • Identify shortage and quality problems along the supply chain

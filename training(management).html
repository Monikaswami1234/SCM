CASE STUDIES ON SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT

In today’s dynamic business environment, effective supply chain management is crucial for operational efficiency, cost reduction, and customer satisfaction. This corporate training program provides a comprehensive understanding of supply chain strategies and best practices through real-life case studies from the industry. Participants will design and analyze Supply chain models, identify challenges, and develop data-driven solutions to optimize their own supply chain processes.

  • Understand supply chain fundamentals & best practices
  • Build Supply Chain Models
  • Analyze real-world supply chain challenges & solutions
  • Develop problem-solving skills through case studies
  • Learn strategies for improving efficiency, reducing costs, and mitigating risk
  • Learn how to manage disruptions and build a resilient supply chain
